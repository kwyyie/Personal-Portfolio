Who is Elijah Coronel?

Elijah Mae B. Coronel is a talented student at Our Lady of Fatima University, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). She is committed to excelling both academically and creatively.

An alumna and consistent honor student, Elijah takes pride in her academic success and dedication to excellence. During her senior high school years, she was a proud member of the EngSoc Editors Team, honing her skills in communication and creativity.

Known for her strong sense of fashion and her expertise in digital design, she blends artistic vision with technical precision to create engaging, impactful designs. With a passion for both aesthetics and functionality, Elijah continues to push boundaries and develop her skills in the world of design.
